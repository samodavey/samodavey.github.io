Final Year Project: This Is My Stop!

"This Is My Stop!" is a 3D third person local multiplayer beat em' up game using standard controller input, this means that multiple cameras will be displayed on the screen showing the perspective for each player character ranging from two to four players.

The game is set within an urban New York City style subway with two main goals for the players. At the start of the game a random team is selected to play as the targeted gang and their main goal is to reach the exit point without having their team leader (the player) eliminated. Meanwhile, all other teams act as the hunters and their main goal is to track down and destroy the targeted gang.

VR Project: Echos Of The Beast

Echos Of The Beast was a virtual reality developed by myself and two other friends whilst at university. The main premise of the game is that you play the role of Theseus in ancient Greece navigating the labyrinth where the Minotaur resides in the dark.

Using a microphone with the VR headset the player must use echolocation to navigate the labyrinth and escape without alerting the Minotaur to your presence!
